This application is intended to provide the fictional company "My Little Cobotics Shop" and its customers with an environment to communicate and execute transactions in a comfortable and fast way.

Here's what you can do on this site:

  • Create a customer account and buy one of our products. We are sure you will be pleased to meet our dear guys: Talisman, Overlord and Sentinel!

  • Create a supplier account and show what your company's got! You will be able to offer your services and when validated, we will purchase them!

  • If you are a member of our company, you can go to the staff login page to log in. Depending on your department, you will be able to carry out some actions or some others. In case you are from the management department and have a high access level, well... You are the boss! You can do whatever: validate services, purchase them, manufacture products, see information about our sales and clients, modify prices and also create new staff users!

This web application was intended to be a merely academic project for the course in Python programming of the academy Tokio School. The name of the company as well as any other name in this app either of the application, products or default users are purely fictional and do not try to represent any real person, institution or organization.

The information required to create users, especially in the case of clients, is particularly personal, such as the credit card number. Though this application will only work on your personal machine, it is strongly recommended not to use any real information.

This web project has been created in 2023. We still have a long way in front of us before we can go on the Internet and buy robots to be our pets, help us or protect us. Then, let's move forward in time and imagine we are some decades ahead...


My Little Cobotics Shop was created to make people's life easier, by counting on intelligent machines able to help them, either with its company as pets, or with their daily tasks or protecting their homes at night. As long as (needless to say) all these points do not enter in conflict with any of Isaac Asimov's Three Laws of Robotics:


1. A robot may not injure a human being or, through inaction, allow a human being to come to harm.


2. A robot must obey the orders given it by human beings, except where such orders would conflict with the First Law.


3. A robot must protect its own existence as long as such protection does not conflict with the First or the Second Laws.


From this point forward, our commitment is clear: we work to bring cobots to reality, making them more intelligent, more helpful and safer day by day.
